Daily Use Notes

These are the simple checks that most affect day-to-day reliability and riding experience.

Keep a healthy charge level

Avoid storing the battery at very low power for long periods.

Check fasteners regularly

Inspect handlebars, brakes, wheels, and other key parts monthly.

Avoid deep water and heavy rain storage

The bike has protection, but deep water, high-pressure washing, and long rain exposure should still be avoided.

FAQ

Quick answers for common pre-purchase and ownership questions.

How should I choose between ES and ES Pro?

Choose ES for shorter daily rides. Choose ES Pro for longer commutes, higher-frequency use, stronger motor output, and dual disc brakes.

Is the battery removable?

The product uses a side-removable battery design. Please follow the user guide or support instructions for removal.

How long does charging take?

ES takes about 2 hours; ES Pro takes about 5.5 hours. Actual time depends on remaining power and environment.

Do you support bulk purchase inquiries?

Yes. Companies, parks, and channel partners can contact sales through the website form or phone.
