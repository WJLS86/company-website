Brand Story About Da Miin

Wuxi Da Miin Bicycle Co., Ltd., an innovative company combining fashion design and riding technology

Brand Concept

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Wuxi, Jiangsu, Wuxi Da Miin Bicycle Co., Ltd. is an innovative company combining fashion design and riding technology.

We create micro electric mobility products with design, personality, and everyday fun, redefining the aesthetics and experience of urban travel.

Founding Team

Our team brings together creators who understand urban culture and lifestyle trends. Core members include London College of Fashion graduates and senior e-bike experts, working with manufacturing partners with 20 years of experience.
